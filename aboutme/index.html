ABOUT ME

My interest in data science started in high school when I discovered a deep affinity for numbers, economic theories, and analytical courses. Later on, my academic journey led me to the Barcelona School of Economics (BSE), where I completed a first Master's degree in economics of energy, sustainability, and climate change. Along this path, my curiosity for data science continuously grew, eventually leading me to pursue a second Master's degree in Data Science for Decision Making.

Beyond the classroom, I've had the privilege of gaining practical experience in a recruitment agency and the Munich Liaison Office of the European Parliament. Throughout this journey, I've witnessed the incredible potential of data science across diverse fields, and I'm thrilled to be a part of its transformative impact. On my website, I eagerly share my experiences, insights, and ongoing projects in the world of data science and economics of energy.

Analyzing

In a wide range of subject areas, I have analyzed structured and unstructured data to extract actionable business insights. I love to create captivating and insightful visual representations that bring out unexpected findings.

Developing

With a strong foundation in data science acquired from my master's degree in data science for decision making, I've enhanced my developing skills to bring machine learning models to life and create impactful, data-driven solutions.

Communicating

I enjoy writing professional articles, sharing my expertise, and engaging in conversations on a wide array of subjects. My background in economic science and management allows me to effectively present complex findings to non-technical audiences
